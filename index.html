POWERFOIL X3.0

THE BEST SELLING BIG FAN IN THE WORLD

Powerfoil X3.0’s superior hub and airfoil systems deliver unmatched airflow to big spaces, powered by a purpose-built NitroSeal™ drive system and supported by world-class safety features. With premium materials and precise engineering, Powerfoil X3.0 offers the custom fit to improve comfort, safety, and efficiency in any application.

THE BEST SELLING BIG FAN IN THE WORLD

  • Best-built, longest lasting, top performing and best selling fan on the planet!
  • Warrantied for 15 years and engineered to last much longer!
  • Most economical HVLS fan on the marketing when full service life is considered.

Perfect Solution For…

  • Customers willing to invest more now to save in the long run
  • Spaces that can’t afford to shut down, such as manufacturing facilities
  • Dirty environments
  • Customers with limited or taxed maintenance crews
  • Applications where equipment, such as lights, cameras, or conduit, can’t be relocated to allow for a fan
  • Customers who own the building or plan on being in the space for at least 5 years
  • Customers who have better things to worry about than keeping a fan spinning. Zero Maintenance = Zero Worries

NOT the Best Solution For….

  • Customers whose main deciding factor is price
  • Customers who simply gather quotes for another decision maker
