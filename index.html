Features

Stober Features

  • Custom engineered by Stober, a German company known for their precision gearing.
  • A perfect partner, Stober’s Maysville, KY factory is only 65 miles from our Lexington factory.

Nitroseal Gearbox

  • The result was the Nitroseal gearbox – the only purpose-engineered gearbox in the industry.
  • Gears are oversized, precision-cut, and perfectly matched to reduce friction, heat, and noise.
  • The cooler operation allows the gearbox to be hermetically sealed (airtight). It is the only non-vented box in the industry.
  • The offset motor allows for field replacement at ceiling level.
  • C-face connector allows motor to be replaced independently of the gearbox
  • Less cost and less downtime
  • Built with the most high-end components available to ensure premium performance, safety, and maximum life
  • Factory-tested at full load prior to shipment
